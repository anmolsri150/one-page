Calamities we cover and precautions

Earthquakes

During earthquake we should drop down and take cover under a desk or a table. Stay indoors untill the shaking stops. Stay away from book cases or furniture taht can fall.

Tsunami's

Tsunami can wreak havoc on coastal population and landscapes. During tsunami warning, plan an evacuation route that leads to higher ground. Never stay near to watch a tsunamic come in.

Floods

Flooding may occur as an overflow of water fromwater bodies such as river, lake or ocean. When a flood is imminent pack a bag with important items in case there is a need to evacuate. If there is a possibility of a flash flood move immediately to higher ground.

Nuclear Disaster

During a Nuclear accident take a safe shelter, take iodine tablets if prescribed and follow the instruction of the authorities.
